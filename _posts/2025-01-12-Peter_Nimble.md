# Peter Nimble and His Fantastic Eyes
### Author: <a href="">Jonathan Auxier</a>
### Rating: 10/10


Peter Nimble is a child with extraorinary capabilities, he is a theif in an ordinary town, when he discovers a box of eyes, he embarks on a journey to find a missing island, and discover secrets about his past.

This book was very well writen, and kept the suspense well, the plot was very interesting and enjoyable, with the intriguing twist of the main protaganist being a theif. In conclusion, I really enjoyed this book and would recomend it to lovers of books
